                      VIRAL COEFFICIENT (K-FACTOR)
================================================================================

  ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
  │                           CURRENT K-FACTOR: 0.75                        │
  │                                                                         │
  │  K = (Avg invites per user) × (Invite conversion rate)                 │
  │  K = 3.0 invites × 25% conversion = 0.75                               │
  └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  ┌──────────────────────┬──────────────────────┬──────────────────────────┐
  │ INVITES/USER         │ CONVERSION RATE      │ CYCLE TIME               │
  ├──────────────────────┼──────────────────────┼──────────────────────────┤
  │       3.0            │       25%            │       14 days            │
  └──────────────────────┴──────────────────────┴──────────────────────────┘

  OPTIMIZATION PATH TO K > 1.0:
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Option A: Increase invites to 4.0 at 25% conversion = K = 1.0
  Option B: Boost conversion to 34% at 3.0 invites = K = 1.02

  TACTICS TO IMPROVE:
  • Prompt for invites at activation moment (after first health insight)
  • Double-sided incentive: 1 month free for referrer AND referee
  • One-click invite with pre-written personalized message
  • Show social proof: "3 of your friends already use PetPulse"
