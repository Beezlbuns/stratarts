A/B Testing Strategy

Subject Line Tests

  • Personalization: "[Pet Name]" vs no pet name
  • Urgency: "Last chance" vs "Don't miss out"
  • Curiosity: Question vs statement
  • Length: Short (30 chars) vs long (50 chars)
  • Emoji: With emoji vs without

Content Tests

  • CTA button color: Green vs blue
  • Email length: Short vs detailed
  • Image placement: Top vs inline
  • Social proof: With testimonial vs without
  • Send time: Morning vs evening

Deliverability Best Practices

List Hygiene

  • Double opt-in for all signups
  • Remove hard bounces immediately
  • Sunset 6-month inactive subscribers
  • Run re-engagement before removal
  • Monitor spam complaint rate (<0.1%)

Authentication

  • SPF record configured
  • DKIM signature enabled
  • DMARC policy set
  • Custom sending domain
  • Warm up new domain slowly

Engagement

  • Segment by engagement level
  • Send less to unengaged users
  • Personalize subject lines
  • Optimize send times by timezone
  • Use preference center
