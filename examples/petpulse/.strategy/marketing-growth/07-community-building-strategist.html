Community Strategy Overview

The PetPulse Pack community will bring together devoted pet parents who want to give their furry family members the best care possible. By combining peer support, health education, and exclusive access, we'll build a community that reduces churn, generates referrals, and creates passionate brand advocates.

Moderation & Community Guidelines

Community Rules

ü§ù
1. Be Respectful & Supportive
Pet parenting is emotional. Support each other, no judgment on pet care choices.
üö´
2. No Medical Diagnosis
Share experiences, not diagnoses. Always recommend consulting a vet for health concerns.
üéØ
3. Stay On Topic
Pet health and PetPulse discussions in main channels. Off-topic in #random.
üö´
4. No Spam or Self-Promotion
No promoting products or services without explicit permission.

Moderation Approach

Philosophy: Light touch with rapid response

Team:

  • 3-4 PetPulse team admins
  • 10-15 Pack Leaders (community mods)
  • 1 mod per 100 active members target

Response Times:

  • Spam: <1 hour (automod)
  • Violations: <4 hours
  • Appeals: <48 hours

Escalation: Warning ‚Üí 24hr timeout ‚Üí 7-day ban ‚Üí Permanent
