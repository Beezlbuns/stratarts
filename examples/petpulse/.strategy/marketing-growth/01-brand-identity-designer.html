Brand Guidelines

Rules for maintaining brand consistency across all touchpoints and communications.

Voice Do's & Don'ts

‚úì Do
  • Use "pet parent" instead of "owner"
  • Lead with benefits, support with features
  • Acknowledge pet parent anxiety with empathy
  • Use contractions (we're, you'll) for warmth
  • Back claims with specific data
  • Celebrate the pet-human bond
‚úó Don't
  • Use fear-based messaging or alarmist language
  • Make medical diagnoses or replace vet advice
  • Use cold, clinical, or overly technical language
  • Dismiss concerns as overreaction
  • Claim "the only" or "the first" without proof
  • Use jargon (AI, ML, IoT) without explanation
