  PILLAR PAGE STRUCTURE
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  □ H1: Primary keyword (exact match)
  □ Meta Title: Primary KW | Brand (55-60 chars)
  □ Meta Description: Primary + secondary KW, CTA (150-160 chars)
  □ H2s: Secondary keywords (4-6 sections)
  □ H3s: Long-tail variations
  □ Internal links: 5+ to supporting content
  □ External links: 2-3 to authoritative sources
  □ Images: 4-6 with descriptive alt text
  □ FAQ section: 5 questions with schema
  □ Word count: 2,500-3,500 words
  □ Table of contents: Linked to H2s

  SUPPORTING ARTICLE STRUCTURE
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  □ H1: Long-tail keyword variation
  □ Meta Title: Target keyword | PetPulse (55-60 chars)
  □ Meta Description: Keyword + benefit + CTA
  □ H2s: 3-4 main sections
  □ Internal links: 3+ (1 to pillar, 2+ to cluster)
  □ Word count: 1,200-1,800 words
  □ CTA: Link to pillar or product page
