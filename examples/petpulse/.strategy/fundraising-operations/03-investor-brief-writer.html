PetPulse

The Problem

Pet parents spend $35K+ on pet healthcare over a pet's lifetime but have no visibility into their pet's health between vet visits. 65% of serious conditions are caught too late, leading to expensive treatments, shorter lifespans, and heartbroken families.

Our Solution

PetPulse is a smart collar + mobile app that continuously monitors your pet's health metrics (heart rate, activity, sleep, temperature) and uses AI to detect health anomalies before they become emergencies. Early detection saves lives and reduces vet bills by 40%.

$260B global pet industry, with pet tech growing at 25% CAGR. 90M U.S. households own pets, spending $136B/year. Our SAM: $4B in pet health monitoring and wearables.
