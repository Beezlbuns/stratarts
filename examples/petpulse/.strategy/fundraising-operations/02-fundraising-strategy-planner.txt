================================================================================
                       MEETING PROGRESSION
================================================================================

    STAGE 1: INTRO MEETING (15-30 min)
    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Who: You + 1 Partner
    Goal: Gauge interest, get to partner meeting
    What to Present: Condensed pitch (10 slides)
    Key Questions: Market size, traction, team background
    Success: "Let's schedule a partner meeting"

    STAGE 2: PARTNER MEETING (45-60 min)
    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Who: You + Full Partnership (3-6 partners)
    Goal: Deep dive, build conviction for DD
    What to Present: Full pitch deck + 20-min Q&A
    Key Questions: Unit economics, competitive moat, roadmap
    Success: "We'd like to move forward with due diligence"

    STAGE 3: DUE DILIGENCE (1-2 weeks)
    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Who: You + Investor team + Stakeholders
    Goal: Validate claims, assess risks
    Activities:
    - Data room review (financials, metrics, contracts)
    - Customer reference calls (5 customers)
    - Technical DD (hardware, app architecture)
    - Back-channel references
    Success: Investor gives term sheet

    STAGE 4: TERM SHEET (1 week)
    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Who: You + Investor + Lawyers
    Goal: Negotiate terms, select lead
    What to Negotiate: Valuation, board, pro-rata, option pool
    Success: Sign term sheet

    STAGE 5: CLOSING (2-4 weeks)
    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Who: You + Investor + Lawyers
    Goal: Legal DD, finalize documents, wire funds
    Activities: SPA, IRA, Voting Agreement, signatures
    Success: Money in bank
