1. Company Foundation

Mission

Empower pet parents to keep their furry family members healthy and happy through continuous, AI-powered health monitoring that catches issues before they become emergencies.

Vision

Become the trusted health companion for every pet family worldwide - the platform that veterinarians recommend and pet parents love, reducing preventable pet health crises by 50% within a decade.

Core Values

Pet Parents First
"We talk to 10 pet parents every week. Every feature starts with their pain points, not our assumptions."
Science-Backed, Vet-Validated
"No feature ships without veterinary review. We earn trust through clinical accuracy, not marketing claims."
Move Fast, Care Deeply
"Ship in days, not months. But never ship something that could harm a pet or mislead a parent."
Own It
"No 'that's not my job.' If you see a problem, you own fixing it until someone else explicitly takes over."
Celebrate the Pack
"We win as a team. Share credit generously, give feedback kindly, and lift each other up."

8. Onboarding Playbook

Pre-Day 1
  • Send welcome email with Day 1 logistics
  • Set up Slack, GitHub, email, and tool accounts
  • Ship laptop and PetPulse collar
  • Add to org chart and relevant Slack channels
Day 1
  • 9am: Welcome meeting with manager (tour, intros, expectations)
  • 10am: IT setup (laptop, accounts, 2FA)
  • 11am: HR paperwork (I-9, benefits, equity docs)
  • 12pm: Team lunch (company pays)
  • 2pm: Company overview from CEO (mission, vision, strategy)
  • 3pm: Product deep-dive with CTO (architecture, roadmap)
  • 4pm: Meet your pet buddy (shadow a customer call)
Week 1
  • Day 2: 1-on-1s with each department head (15 min each)
  • Day 3: Read key docs (strategy, product roadmap, OKRs)
  • Day 4: Set up your own pet on PetPulse (dogfooding)
  • Day 5: First small project assigned (with mentor pairing)
Week 2-4
  • Week 2: Ship first meaningful contribution (code, content, or deal)
  • Week 3: Talk to 5 customers (understand their pet health journeys)
  • Week 4: Present learnings to team (what surprised you?)
Day 30
  • 30-Day review with manager (what's working, what's not)
  • Feedback survey (how was onboarding? what can we improve?)
  • Set 90-day goals with manager
