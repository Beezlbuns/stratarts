6. Meeting Progression

What happens at each stage of the investor relationship

1

Intro Meeting (15-30 min)

Condensed pitch (10 slides), gauge interest, get to partner meeting. Key questions: market size, traction, team background.

2

Partner Meeting (45-60 min)

Full pitch deck + deep Q&A with entire partnership. Unit economics deep dive, competitive moat, product roadmap, fundraising history.

3

Due Diligence (1-2 weeks)

Data room access, customer reference calls (5 customers), technical DD (hardware manufacturing, app architecture), back-channel references.

4

Term Sheet (1 week)

Negotiate valuation, board seat, pro-rata rights, liquidation preference. Target: 2-3 term sheets for leverage.

5

Closing (2-4 weeks)

Legal DD, document drafting (SPA, IRA, Voting Agreement), final signatures, wire transfer, cap table update, announcement.

1x Non-Participating
Standard, founder-friendly. Reject 2x or participating preferred.
Option Pool
15% Post-Money
Standard for Series A. Sufficient for 2-year hiring plan (12 roles).
Pro-Rata Rights
Major Investors Only
Pro-rata for investors with >5% ownership. Helps ensure follow-on support.
Vesting
4yr / 1yr Cliff
Standard vesting. Credit existing time served if negotiated well.

9. Closing Process

4-week timeline from term sheet to wire

Week 1
Sign Term Sheet

Select lead investor, negotiate final terms, sign binding term sheet. Announce to other interested investors (create urgency for follow-on).

Week 2
Legal Due Diligence

Investor lawyers review contracts, IP assignments, employment agreements. Address any issues (cap table cleanup, contract amendments).

Week 3
Document Drafting

Draft Stock Purchase Agreement (SPA), Investor Rights Agreement (IRA), Voting Agreement, ROFR/Co-Sale Agreement.

Week 4
Signatures & Wire

All parties sign final documents. Investor wires $5M. Issue stock certificates, update cap table. Board meeting to ratify.

Post-Close
Announce & Thank

Press release, blog post, social media announcement. Thank all investors who participated. Send update to investors who passed (maintain relationships).
