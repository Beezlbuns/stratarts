Pricing Psychology

Psychological tactics applied to optimize conversion and perceived value.

🎯
Decoy Effect
Essential tier at $19 makes Premium at $29 feel like exceptional value with telehealth + AI included.
$10/mo more for 5x the features creates clear upgrade path
⚓
Price Anchoring
Premium Plus at $49 anchors perception, making Premium at $29 feel reasonable for premium pet parents.
Display tiers left-to-right: Essential, Premium, Premium Plus
📊
Annual Discount
17% annual discount improves cash flow and reduces churn by creating commitment.
$290/year vs $348 monthly = save $58
💰
Value Framing
Frame $29/mo as "less than $1/day" for peace of mind about your pet's health.
"Less than your daily coffee for 24/7 pet health monitoring"
