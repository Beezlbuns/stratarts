90-Day Tactical Roadmap

Week-by-week execution plan with specific tasks, milestones, and success metrics. Each month has a theme that guides tactical priorities.

Month 1: Foundation & Launch
Goal: 650 new subscribers, $29K MRR
Week 1-2
  • Launch Facebook/Instagram ad campaigns (5 creative variants)
  • Onboard first 5 pet influencers with product samples
  • Set up analytics tracking (Meta Pixel, Google Analytics)
Week 3-4
  • Optimize ad campaigns based on first week data
  • Publish first 6 SEO blog posts
  • Launch referral program with $25/$25 incentive
Month 2: Optimization & Scale
Goal: 850 new subscribers, $50K MRR
Key Tactics
  • Scale winning ad creatives, pause underperformers
  • Launch Cat Support product line with dedicated campaigns
  • Onboard 5 additional micro-influencers
  • Launch pet health symptom checker (lead gen tool)
Month 3: Acceleration & Expansion
Goal: 1,000 new subscribers, $75K MRR
Key Tactics
  • Increase ad budget to $30K (winning channels)
  • Launch first macro-influencer campaign (500K+ followers)
  • Begin insurance partnership outreach
  • PR push: Target pet industry publications

Risks & Mitigation

Key risks that could derail the GTM plan with specific mitigation strategies.

High CAC on Paid Ads
Facebook/Instagram CAC could exceed $50 due to competitive pet market.
Mitigation: Test 10 creative variants in week 1, shift budget to influencer channel if CAC >$40 by week 3.
Influencer Underperformance
Influencer partnerships may not drive expected conversions.
Mitigation: Use micro-influencers first (lower cost, higher engagement), track with unique codes, pause non-performers quickly.
Supply Chain Delays
Hardware collar inventory could run out during scale-up.
Mitigation: Pre-order 5,000 units, establish waitlist system if demand exceeds supply.
