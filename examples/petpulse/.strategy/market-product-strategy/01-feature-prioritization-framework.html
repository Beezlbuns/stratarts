Deferred & Killed Features

Features deprioritized or removed from the roadmap with clear rationale. Being ruthless about what NOT to build is as important as choosing what to build.

‚è∏Ô∏è Deferred Features

International Expansion (UK/Canada)
Why Deferred: Requires regulatory compliance for vet telehealth in each country, different vet licensing requirements. Focus on US market first to prove model.
Revisit: Q1 2027 after reaching 50K US subscribers
Apple Watch Companion App
Why Deferred: Limited user demand (only 12% of target market). Pet parents prefer phone-based alerts. Low confidence in ROI.
Revisit: Q3 2027 if wearable adoption increases

‚ùå Killed Features

Pet Social Network / Community Features
Why Killed: Distracts from core health value proposition. Pet parents want health tools, not another social network. Would require significant moderation resources. Competitors like BarkHappy already failed in this space.
Gamification / Achievement Badges
Why Killed: Premium pet parents view health monitoring as serious, not a game. Gamification trivializes the value proposition. Beta feedback was negative (3.2/10 interest score).
