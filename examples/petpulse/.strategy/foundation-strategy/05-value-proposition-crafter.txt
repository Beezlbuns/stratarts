  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  ACKNOWLEDGE: "GPS trackers are great for knowing where your pet is."

  REFRAME:     "But do they tell you HOW your pet is? Health issues don't
               start with location — they start with subtle changes in
               activity, sleep, and vital signs."

  EVIDENCE:    "PetPulse detected health anomalies in 15,000+ pets during

  BRIDGE:      "Think of PetPulse as the health counterpart to your GPS —
               location + health = complete peace of mind."

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  OBJECTION 3: "My vet is only 10 minutes away — I don't need telehealth."
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  ACKNOWLEDGE: "That's great that you have a vet nearby."

  REFRAME:     "But how long does it take to get an appointment? Most non-
               emergency visits are 2-3 weeks out. And what about 2am when
               your pet is acting strange?"

  EVIDENCE:    "PetPulse telehealth has a 4-minute average response time,
               24/7. 68% of consultations are outside normal vet hours."

  BRIDGE:      "We're not replacing your vet — we're giving you expert
               guidance for those 'should I be worried?' moments in between."
