Common Objections & Responses

Anticipated objections with strategic response frameworks.

"My pet is healthy, I don't need this."
Response Strategy
Share statistics: 67% of pet health issues are detected late. PetPulse caught early signs of heart disease in 23% of "healthy" dogs during beta. Prevention saves $3,000+ in emergency vet costs. Frame as insurance, not treatment.
"I already have a GPS tracker / fitness collar."
Response Strategy
Acknowledge: GPS trackers are great for location. PetPulse is different - it's a health monitor + instant vet access. Whistle/Fi track activity; we monitor vital signs (heart rate, temperature, respiratory) AND connect you to vets in 60 seconds.
"What if my dog won't wear it?"
Response Strategy
30-day happiness guarantee - full refund if your dog doesn't love it. 94% of dogs adjust within 3 days. Collar weighs only 28g (lighter than a treat). Share video testimonials of dogs wearing collar comfortably.
